If the probability is 0.54 that Stock A will increase in value during the next month and the probability is 0.68 that Stock B will increase in value during the next month, what is the greatest possible value for the probability that neither of these two events will occur?
A.0.22
B.0.32
C.0.37
D.0.46
E.0.63

we know that A and B are not independent events because 0.54+0.68=1.22>1

draw venn diagram, two circles overlapping, and the number (let it be x) outside both circles is the probability that neither of these two events will occur. x is greatest when the numbers inside the circles and the overlapping part are 0, 0.54, 0.14.

So x=1-0.54-0.14=0.32.
