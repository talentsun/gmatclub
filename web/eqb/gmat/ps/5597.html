Before being simplified, the instructions for computing income tax in Contry R were to add 2 percent of one's annual income to the average (arithmetic mean) of 100 unit of Contry R's currency and 1 percent of one's annual income. Which of the following represents the simplified formula for computing the income tax, in Country R's currency, for a person in that whose annual income is I?
A.50 + I/200
B.50 + 3I/100
C.50 + I/40
D.100 + I/50
E.100 + 3I/100

这一题其实不难，只不过题目古怪，也出得不好。R 国的所得税是这样计算的：年薪收入的 2%，再加上年薪收入 1% 与 100 元的平均.

步骤是I*2%+(100+1%*I)/2=50+I/40
