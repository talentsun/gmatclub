The rate of a certain chemical reaction is directly proportaional to the square of the concentration of chemical A present and inversely proportional to the concentration of chemical B present. If the concentration of chemical B is increased by 100%, which of the following is closest to the percent change in the concentration of chemical A required to keep the reaction rate unchanged?
A.100% decrease
B.50% decrease
C.40% decrease
D.40% increase
E.50% increase

关键词：
rate速度，proportional成比例的，concentration浓度，inversely相反地
解题过程：
反应速率＝k*(A^2)*(1/B)
B上升100%后，B'＝（1+100%）B＝2B
要反应速度不变，需要k*(A^2)*(1/B)＝k*(A'^2)*(1/B')
k约掉，B'代入，(A^2)*(1/B)＝(A'^2)*(1/2B)
B约掉，A’^2=2*(A^2)
两边开根号，A'=根号2*A
根号2＝1.414，故上涨40%
