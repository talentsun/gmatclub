In an increasing sequence of 10 consecutive integers, the sum of the first 5 integers is 560. What is the sum of the last 5 integers in the sequence?
A.585
B.580
C.575
D.570
E.565

连续的10个整数，a,a+1,a+2,a+3,a+4是前5个，和为560；

后5个：a+5,a+6,a+7,a+8,a+9 ，其中a+5比a 大5， a+6比a+1大5，以此类推，后5个数相应比前5个各自都多5, 5*5=25，因此，后5个数之和=560+25=585
