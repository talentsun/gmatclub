For every positive even integer n, the function h(n) is defined to be the product of all the even integers from 2 to n, inclusive. If p is the smallest prime factor of h(100) + 1, then p is
A.between 2 and 10
B.between 10 and 20
C.between 20 and 30
D.between 30 and 40
E.greater than 40

因为h(n)+1 = 2*4*…*n +1 = 2^(n/2) * (1*2*3*4*…*n/2) + 1

设S = 2^(n/2) * (1*2*3*4*…*n/2)

显然S是2，3，5，7，…, n的倍数

这里拿3做个例子，S肯定能表示成3k （其中k为正整数），那么

h(n)+1 = 3k+1 必然不是3的倍数，那么同理S也能表示成5k，7k，…, nk等形式，所以h（n）+1也同样可以表示成5k+1, 7k+1,…, (n/2)k+1,所以h(n)+1也不是5，7，…,n/2等的倍数

综上，h(100)+1不是3，5，7，…, 41, 47的倍数，所以选E
