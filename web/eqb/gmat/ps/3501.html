For a nonnegative integer n, if the remainder is 1 when
A.I only
B.II only
C.I and II
D.I and III
E.II and III

当 n=0 时，2^0=1, 1除以3，商0余1. Therefore, n does not have be greater than zero.

Eliminating I leaves us option B and E, which means, II must be true. If II must be true, n must be an even integer for 3^n=(-3)^n. Thus, when n is an even interger, even if n=0, III is true.
