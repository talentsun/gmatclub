The primary purpose of the passage is to
A.present an interpretation of the chronological relationship between bipedal locomotion and certain other key aspects of human evolution
B.compare the evolutionary advantages and disadvantages of bipedal locomotion to those of quadrupedal locomotion
C.argue that the transition to a nuclear family structure was a more crucial step in human evolution than was the development of stone tools
D.analyze anatomical evidence of bipedal locomotion to show that the large brain of modern humans developed at a later stage of evolution than was previously believed
E.use examples of muscle and bone structure to illustrate the evolutionary differences between modern humans, australopithecines, and chimpanzees

此题属于文章主旨题。此文是旧观点-->新观点-->解释新观点 这样的一个结构。为什么？

第一段的第一句就明白的说了Anthropologists once thought 。。。就可以看出作者在陈述一个过去人们认同的观点或理解。在gmat文章中，以这样的句式开头的，很多时候就是以旧观点-->新观点-->解释新观点 这样的一个结构展开的。

明白了这个结构之后，看看选项：

B 一看compare了locomotion就可以知道这是一个细节，不会是purpose，pass;

C 看关键词，nuclear family只出现在最后一段，是进一步陈述新观点的一个例子，细节，pass;

D 看关键词 large brain developed 更晚。这个问题出现在全文关注的是walk这个行为，lomocation，而大脑有关的问题只是随便提到。从全文第一句话可以看出来：brain of increased size只是处于一个从句的地位。

E 看关键to illustate the 。。。differences .细节，pass

A选项：present an interpreation 这个表述并不重要，关键是后面：

chronological relationship between bipedal locomotion and certain other key aspects of human evolution

首先， chronological relationship ，此文的旧观点之所以被 challenge是因为有了一个fossil，这个fossil在时间上能说明些事情 ，从第一段的最后一句中的prior to 这里就可以看出：that upright walking appeared prior to the dramatic enlargement of the brain and the development of stone tools.文中提到了各种对比和有关现象的解释，目的都是为了说明这个。找到了这个topic sentence，一切都清楚了。
