The passage suggests that Jarvis’s work has called into question which of the following explanatory variables for naked mole rat behavior?
A.Size
B.Age
C.Reproductive status
D.Rate of growth
E.Previously exhibited behavior

题目中说Jarvis’s work 使得下面那个选项出现了问题，而在文中Jarvis’s work has suggested that differences in growth rates may influence the length of time that an individual performs a task, regardless of its age.说明年龄与完成任务的时间无关，因此假设年龄的条件发生了质疑，因此选择B

此题考得是作题人的仔细程度！如果眼睛一晃，忽略了call into question而想当然的人为是问提出了哪一个观点的话，就会选错咯！
