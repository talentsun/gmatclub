The main purpose of the passage is to
A.propose an experiment
B.analyze a function
C. refute an argument
D.explain a contradiction
E.simulate a process

这道题的关键是要看清作者的观点，而作者在最后一段中写道To accept Searle's argument, one would have to deny the most fundamental notion in psychology and neuroscience: that brains work by processing information.如果接受了Searle's 的观点，就要违背心理学和神经科学的基本论述：大脑是靠传递信息工作的。因此作者是反驳Searle的argument的。因此选择C
