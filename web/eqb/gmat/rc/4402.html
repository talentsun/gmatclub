It can be inferred from the passage that if the S waves from an earthquake arrive at a given location long after the P waves, which of the following must be true?
A.The earthquake was a deep event.
B.The earthquake was a shallow event.
C.The earthquake focus was distant.
D.The earthquake focus was nearby.
E.The earthquake had a low peak intensity.

A 和C存在争议。但是A更好

因为the interval between their arrivals increases in proportion to the distance from the earthquake focus, or initial rupture point.
是到earthquake focus 距离增加，而不是earthquake focus 本身在深处，所以C不对

 

另外，从Both the P-S intervals and the intensity patterns suggested two kinds of earthquakes可以看出，因为用的是both...and,所以单单据 P-S intervals 就可以推断出deep event,所以A是对的
