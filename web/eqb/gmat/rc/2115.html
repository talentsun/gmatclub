According to the passage, a hot spot on Earth's surface is an indication of which of the following?
A.An untapped reservoir of hot rock in the base of the mantle
B.Volcanic activity at the edge of a plate
C.Solid mantle rock under tremendous pressure
D.The occurrence of a phenomenon unique to the Pacific plate
E.A plume of hot mantle rock originating near Earth's core

1、这题属于supporting ideas。根据OG11的解释：the phrase "according to the passage" indicates that the necessary information is explicitly stated in the passage.也就是说答案基本上可以在原文找到，不需要绕弯，只不过表述的方式稍有不同而已。

2、看文章这一句：The surface mark of an established plume is a hot spot(一个已经形成的地柱的地表特征是a hot spot),换句话就是说a hot spot on earth's surface is an indication of an established plume.

3、选A的战友很大程度上是因为没有充分理解OG对According to the passage这类题的难度要求，做题时复杂化了。
