According to the passage, Kerber argued that political leaders thought that the form of government adopted by the United States after the American Revolution depended on which of the following for its success?
A.Women assuming the sole responsibility for instilling political virtue in children
B.Girls becoming the primary focus of a reformed educational system that emphasized political virtue
C.The family serving as one of the primary means by which children were imbued with political virtue
D.The family assuming many of the functions previously performed by schools and churches
E.Men and women assuming equal responsibility for the management of schools, churches, and the family

此题问题所问有点长 看问题找关键词 应该用form of government来定位 其实刚才我们也将第二段读了 应该是没有这部分的内容 所以基本就在第段找 定位到这句A virtuous citizenry was considered essential to the success of the country's republican form of government; virtue was to be instilled not only by churches and schools, but by families, where the mother's role was crucial.后面一句是Thus, 打头 那就再看看吧 总共两句 看第一句 A virtuous citizenry was considered essential to the success of the country's republican form of government;这个见容很虚吧 没什么好记的 后面一句记住 virtue 不仅是被学校和教堂所灌输还有家庭 而在家庭中母亲是非常重要的地位 后面一句其实内容和前面一句差不多 所以也就不用记 看选项

A女性承担唯一的责任在灌输小孩virtue上 此选项所说内容和原文没什么关系吧

B女性成为主要的焦点在这个教育系统中 应该来说这个选项的内容是原文没提到过的 原文只是说母亲在家庭中地位是很重要的 有点偷梁换柱的感觉 这是出题老头常用招数

C家庭有这样一个很重要的地位在灌输小孩virtue 方面 这个选项应该和我们所读到相关吧 所以先留下

D家庭承担了之前学校和教堂的工作 这没吧 原文并没说教堂和学校从此不灌输了吧

E男人都不管了 教育都给女人干 原文有这么说吗

所以只有C 老头经常会出这样的迷惑选项 偷梁换柱 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

对A选项，如果没有sole（单独的，唯一的）就是对的，一字之差；原文是说“virtue was to be instilled not only by churches and schools, but by families, where the mother's role was crucial. ”母亲的角色是“cruicial”而不是“sole”；因为还有churches and schools, 还有父亲。
