Defense attorneys have occasionally argued that their clients’ misconduct stemmed from a reaction to something ingested, but in attributing criminal or delinquent behavior to some food allergy, the perpetrators are in effect told that they are not responsible for their actions.
A.in attributing criminal or delinquent behavior to some food allergy
B.if criminal or delinquent behavior is attributed to an allergy to some food
C.in attributing behavior that is criminal or delinquent to an allergy to some food
D.if some food allergy is attributed as the cause of criminal or delinquent behavior
E.in attributing a food allergy as the cause of criminal or delinquent behavior

搞懂逻辑最重要。这句话的意思是说辩护律师偶尔会把他们的辩护人的行为不检归源于对他们吞咽下去的东西的一个反应，但是如果犯罪或者过失行为被归因于食物过敏的话，犯罪者实际上就会被告知他们不用对他们的行为负责。

 知道句意之后就很好选了。ACE都是 in attributing 开头，逻辑上应该是律师把他们的过失行为归因于对食物过敏，因此主语应该是 defense attorneys, 但是句子中后面跟的主语是 the perpetrators，不对，所以排除。

BD两个选项中，A 明显比C 简洁清楚，所以选 B 选项。
