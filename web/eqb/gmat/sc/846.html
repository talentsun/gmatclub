B.Martha Matilda Harper created America's first retail franchise network, a radical new business model that enabled ownership by women in the late nineteenth and early twentieth centuries of
C.Martha Matilda Harper's creation of America's first retail franchise network was a radical new business model that enabled women in the late nineteenth and early twentieth centuries to own

      A. 正确，逻辑主语为Martha，和句子主语一致，合理;同位语的that从句内部，enable somebody to do x and (to) do y是正确的习语，且to own and (to) gain平行对称。

　　B. That定语从句内enabled ownership by women表达笨拙;enabled+名词无法和句子未划线部分的不定式 and(to)gain financial independence并列。

　　C. Born and sent的逻辑主语只能是Martha本人，而不能是“创造”这一抽象名词;以creation做主语，使句子逻辑意思不合理。

　　D. Network做主语，句子逻辑意思不合理。

　　E. 同D选项，network做主语，句子逻辑意思不合理;enabled+名词无法和句子未划线部分的不定式 and(to)gain financial independence并列。
