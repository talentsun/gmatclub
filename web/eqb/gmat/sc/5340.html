Devastated by the catastrophic plane crash, the residents of Cook County accused the Federal Aviation Administration of failing to warn the pilots that the area was experiencing deadly wind shears during the prior weeks.
A.was experiencing deadly wind shears
B.has experienced deadly wind shears
C.experienced deadly wind shears
D.has been experiencing deadly wind shears
E.had been experiencing deadly wind shears

首先看到during the prior weeks确定用完成时态， 排除A，C。 原句的accused是过去时态，所以在后面的从句中应用过去完成时来表示在说话先前的几个星期就已经完成的动作。
