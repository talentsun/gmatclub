Traffic safety officials predict that drivers will be equally likely to exceed the proposed speed limit as the current one.
A.equally likely to exceed the proposed speed limit as
B.equally likely to exceed the proposed speed limit as they are
C.equally likely that they will exceed the proposed speed limit as
D.as likely that they will exceed the proposed speed limit as
E.as likely to exceed the proposed speed limit as they are

先拆开看：

 drivers will be likely to exceed the proposed speed limit

 drivers are likey to exceed the current speed limit .

合并后 暗喜 真理果然掌握在少数人手中。  

 drivers will be as likely to exceed the proposed speed limit as to exceed the current one.

然后惊喜的发现 没有答案，于是我们郁闷了，但其实proposed speed limit 更抑郁，我都是已经proposed 的老大级身份了 怎么和没有proposed 的speed limit小跟班 一样的地位。

我们把未来忽视了，这还含着wil 跟current 的对比。驾驶员超过未来新规定速度的概率和超过现在规定速度的概率是一样的。所以合并以后是

drivers will be as likely to exceed the proposed speel limit as they are likey to exceed the current one .

于是 E 诞生了，不考虑省略很突兀的出现as they are the current one 确实能导致难产。
