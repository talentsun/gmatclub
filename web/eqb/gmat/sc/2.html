Although a surge in retail sales have raised hopes that there is a recovery finally under way, many economists say that without a large amount of spending the recovery might not last.
A.have raised hopes that there is a recovery finally
B.raised hopes for there being a recovery finally
C.had raised hopes for a recovery finally being
D.has raised hopes that a recovery is finally
E.raised hopes for a recovery finally

A． 主语是a surge 并非 sales ，谓语应该用单数has，故可排除A；there is a 多余，使finally错误地修饰recovery，finally应该修饰under way而不是recovery，是“复苏终于开始”而不是“终于复苏”；

B． For there being的表达不简洁；raised时态错，under way的意为“已经开始并进行或正在进行中”，raised表示已经完成；

C． Had时态错，句中没有强调“过去的过去”，而且raise还在持续；being多余；

D． Correct；

E． raised ；finally应该修饰under way而不是recovery，是“…复苏终于开始”而不是“终于复苏”；
