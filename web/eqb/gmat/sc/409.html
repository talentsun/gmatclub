As Charles Darwin observed, natural selection operates whenever individuals of one genetic composition are better at reproducing than that of others.
A.that of others
B.the other
C.another
D.those of another
E.that of the next

注意that的指代以及other与that的前后对应。

此题是人（individual）之间的比较，属性是one genetic composition，故than后面应该接表示人的词，of 后面接属性即another genetic composition。可知选D
