Despite the recent election of a woman to the office of prime minister, the status of women in Pakistan is little changed from how it was in the last century.
A.is little changed from how it was
B.is a little change from how it was
C.has changed little
D.has changed little from how it has been
E.is little changed from the way it was

 

 A.little作为状语应该在change之后；from how it was累赘

 B.a little change改变了原句的意思，原句表达的是status be changed，根据保持原句的原则，应该保留被动；from how it was显得累赘

 C.正确，原句中in the last century中的last表示的是“最近的”，而不是上一个，所以应该用完成时;C选项简洁有效

 D.错误同B

 E.little作为状语应该在change之后；不够简洁

 

补充：last 此处意思为"最近的"而不是"上一个",否则就不必用the.因此要用现在完成时。

        eg.Did you see the game on TV last night?   昨天晚上

               Interest in golf has grown rapidly in the last ten years.   最近十年

            Things have changed since the last time (=the most recent occasion) you were here.

               I hadn't seen him since the last meeting.
