Cash flows to stock and bond mutual funds have gained strength in the last tow months,but fund managers have not been eager to invest the new money,instead of preferring to raise the cash levels in their portfolios at the new money in six months.
A.have not been eager to invest the new money,instead of preferring to raise the cash levels in their portfolios at
B.have not been eager to invest the new money,instead preferring to raise the cash levels in their portfolios to
C.have not been eager at investing the new money,instead of preferring to raise the cash levels in their portfolios to
D.were not eager to invest the new money,instead of preferring to raise the cash levels in their portifolios at
E.were not eager at investing the new money,instead of preferring to raise the cash levels in their portifolios to

读懂句子的意思很重要：过去两个月来相互基金在股票/债券的现金流得以加强，但基金经理们并不急于进行新的投资，而更愿意将其投资组合中现金的水平提高到六个月的新高。

instead of虽然看起来很合规矩，但其实用在这里不合理，前面并没有doing...的形式；而B中的instead用做副词，整个instead preferring...修饰fund managers。raise ... to而不应该是at。

A和D错在对level的理解上，按照朗文字典的解释

at the ...level表示同一级别

to the ...level是表示达到一个什么样的水平，因此排除A，D加上2楼的原因，排除CE， 就只能选B了
