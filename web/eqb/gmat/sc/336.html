Several financial officers of the company spoke on condition that they not be named in the press reports.
A.that they not be named
B.that their names will not be used
C.that their names are not used
D.of not having their names
E.of not naming them

A正确是因为虚拟语气condition that后面should+动词原形，should省略。

B选项的时态不正确，will在on condition that从句中和主句的spoke无法构成时态一致，并且也没有任何证据表明这里需要使用将来时。

C选项的错误同B，这里没有任何证据表明要使用一般现在时

DE选项使用了on condition of加doing的形式，这个形式应该使用on condition that从句加以替换。

on condition that后面用虚拟语气

A为正确选项。
