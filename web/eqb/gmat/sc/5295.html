The area west of Springfield was a smelting factory from 1879 until 1962，when it was purchased by Manfred Otterbein, the noted philanthropist and animal lover, who established a wildlife refuge on the property and used one of the existing buildings as a rare bird sanctuary.
A.who established a wildlife refuge on the property and used one of the existing buildings as
B.who, establishing a wildlife refuge on the property, used one of the existing buildings like
C.who, when he had established a wildlife refuge on the property, used one of the existing buildings like
D.who had established a wildlife refuge on the property, using one of the existing buildings to be
E.establishing a wildlife refuge on the property and using one of the existing buildings as

文中说S地的西边原来是个臭工厂，再被一个MO这个人买过来以后，在这块地上建了野生动物保护所，并且用一个已存在的建筑作为珍稀鸟类的避难所。

文中establish和 use 是两个由MO 发出的动作， 应用平行结构,排除BD,

作为珍稀鸟类避难所，用as，BC错误;

D项用了had established，表示是在前面purchased发生之前，不对。应该是先买再建，时态也错误。

E 项使用了establishing和using，和前面未划线句子的过去时态不一致。排除。

A 正确。
