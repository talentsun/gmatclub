Legislation in the Canadian province of Ontario requires of both public and private employers that pay be the same for jobs historically held by women as for jobs requiring comparable skill that are usually held by men.
A.that pay be the same for jobs historically held by women as for jobs requiring comparable skill that are
B.that pay for jobs historically held by women should be the same as for a job requiring comparable skills
C.to pay the same in jobs historically held by women as in jobs of comparable skill that are
D.to pay the same regardless of whether a job was historically held by women or is one demanding comparable skills (A)
E.to pay as much for jobs historically held by women as for a job demanding comparable skills

A中，pay在这里作名词，表示“薪水”，require of sb that 名词+should（可省略）+动词原形/be。而且the same for ... as for ...结构对称。正确。

C中，付给...的钱，一般用pay ... for 来表示，这里用了pay ... in。而且 A of B 表示的是一种 A属于B的关系，jobs of comparable skill 表示jobs属于comparable skill，逻辑上不合理。

E中，一般是require sb. to do 这里用了require of sb. to do 不恰当。而且a job demanding comparable skills usually held by men，两个分词短语修饰job容易产生歧义，让人们不知道后一个held by men是修饰job的还是skills的。
