Navigators have known for thousands of years that the ocean has variable currents, but it is only in the last half century that
A.a reasonably clear picture has emerged of the patterns and causes of ocean currents
B.a reasonably clear picture of the patterns of ocean currents and their causes have emerged
C.a reasonably clear picture emerged of ocean currents, their patterns and the causes of them
D.there have emerged a reasonably clear picture of the patterns of ocean currents and what caused them
E.there had emerged a reasonably clear picture of the patterns of ocean currents and their causes

之前狠狠想了一整个冬天也没明白这题选什么A，要什么自行车，最终被大神一句话点醒……

我大匆匆，喵，给跪了。

先让我们选出正确选项来：

A  正确，先排除不看。

B  a reasonably clear picture（单） 和 have emerged（复）不搭配，GG

C  怪段的句子，没有emerge of这种用法，them指代不清，有可能指代patterns，也可能是currents也可能两者都是，GG

D  这么奇葩的句子……喵……there无指代

E   同D

我们来看这个奇怪的A选项，首先我们要明确亮点，一、没有emerge of这种用法，最多有emerge from sth, 从哪里出来，所以还想着of 和 of平行的同学请自戳双目；二、a picture of patterns(图案)是说得通的，a picture of causes of ocean currents是不科学的，我大科学家们怎么可能画图来解释水流的原因。

接着我们就会发现，这是个倒装句：a reasonably clear picture of the patterns has emerged，good，这一部分解决了，后面的是什么东西呢？老师告诉我们，所谓能省略的就是完全相同的所谓后面省略了have emerged。也就是补全了以后是： a reasonably clear picture of the patterns has emerged and causes of ocean currents have emerged. 为了简明，后半句省略。
