The use of lie detectors is based on the assumption that lying produces emotional reactions in an individual that, in turn, create unconscious physiological responses.
A.that, in turn, create unconscious physiological responses
B.that creates unconscious physiological responses in turn
C.creating, in turn, unconscious physiological responses
D.to create, in turn, physiological responses that are unconscious
E.who creates unconscious physiological responses in turn

OG: 划线部分的that引导从句是修饰的reactions，因此create应该是复数形式。排除BE。

A BINGO；In turn一般表示前一个动作 的影响产生了后一个动作，一般放在后一个动作之前，表示出清晰的先后顺序。Create 跳过in an individual 修饰reactions ,简短的介词短语是可以跳过的

B creates 不能修饰复数reactions;  如果是修饰individual不能用that； in turn 的放置无意义，应该放在create 之前表达因果关系

C creating可以优先做伴随状语修饰Lying，还可以作定语修饰individual ，甚至还可以修饰reactions 所以在此处有歧义； 如果想用分词ing ，在这里必须有逗号，但是逻辑上也不合理

D to create不知道是修饰谁的，在这无意义，to do 表目的，在这里显然不是; responses that are unconscious经典啰嗦

E in turn 的放置无意义，应该放在create 之前表达因果关系;  who creates 修饰了 individual不合逻辑, individual不能create responses.
