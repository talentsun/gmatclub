A 1972 agreement between Canada and the United States reduced the amount of phosphates that municipalities had been allowed to dump into the Great Lakes.
A.reduced the amount of phosphates that municipalities had been allowed to dump
B.reduced the phosphate amount that municipalities had been dumping
C.reduces the phosphate amount municipalities have been allowed to dump
D.reduced the amount of phosphates that municipalities are allowed to dump
E.reduces the amount of phosphates allowed for dumping by municipalities

根据句子的意思 肯定时态上只能是过去式比较合理 所以杀CE 而D的are allowed to dump明显也不对 再看AB 意思上是有差别的 最明显的应该是后面的 allowed to 句意上应该是有 allowed to 更为合理的

以上分析认为A正确。答案是D.以下解释D项。

划线靠后，只看前面。reduced和reduces本身没什么考点，都正确，它提示时态搭配是考点。BC和另外三个不同，但the amount of phosphates 优于 the phosphate amount。E的allowed for dumping by municipalities的by 可以修饰 dumping 也可以修饰 allowed 修饰级别混乱。AD的不同在于be allowed 的时态搭配。be allowed的反义词是be forbidden, 所以A的意思是在reduced发生以后就被forbidden了，而D的意思是reduced之后还被allowed，这个现在时态和前面的谓语reduced存在时间轴跳步嫌疑，不过主语是两国政府间的协定，这个现在时态是法律用法。通过句意判断D项最优，问题最少。
