The acrimony in Washington, communicated to the general public to a large degree in sound-bite epithets from the principals on evening television newscasts,
A.contributed to a sour mood between the electorate
B.contributing to a sour mood between the electorate
C.contributed to a sour mood among the electorate
D.contributing to a sour mood among the electorate
E.contributing to a sour mood inside the electorate

The acrimony in Washington, communicated to the general public to a large degree in sound-bite epithets from the principals on evening television newscasts, contributed to a sour mood between the electorate.

首先找主干，句子的主谓-----CONTRIBUTE做谓语。如果是contributing 的话句子就是一个fraction的。所以B, D, E错。

electorate 〉2. 所以要用among. between 是两两之间的。 A错。

答案：C
