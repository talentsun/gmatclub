Pablo Picasso, the late Spanish painter, credited African art with having had a strong influence on his work.
A.with having had
B.for its having
C.to have had
D.for having
E.in that it had

 这个是一位外国友人解释的credit with/credit for的用法：

credit SB with STH (verb): give responsibility for. Thomas Edison is credited with inventing the light bulb.

credit for (noun): money received for or in exchange for something. The customer received a $20 credit for the interruption in service.

credit for 只跟交易和钱有关，本题中完全没有这个意思，所以选A固定搭配。
