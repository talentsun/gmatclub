The Environmental Protection Agency frequently puts mandatory controls on toxic substances that present as little risk as one in a million chances to cause cancer.
A.as little risk as one in a million chances to cause
B.as little risk as one chance in a million of causing
C.as little risk as one chance in a million that it will cause
D.a risk as little as one chance in a million for causing
E.a risk as little as one chance in a million for it to cause

　　句子结构：The EPA puts controls on toxic substances that…

　　That引导定语从句修饰句子宾语substances。

　　考点：习惯用语、指代一致

　　习惯用语-risk of doing sth, risk of sth(risk分别为名词), risk doing(risk为动词)。

　　指代一致，it只能指代句子中出现过的单数名词，注意未划线部分的提示。

　　(A) one指代risk，risk in a million chances表达错误;risk of doing是习惯用语。

　　(B) 正确，one chance in a million用以修饰little，risk of causing cancer是正确的表达方式。

　　(C) that it重复，前面已经有that present，it指代不清楚，无法指代复数名词toxic substances。

　　(D) risk后面应该跟of doing，而不是for doing。

　　(E) it指代不清楚;risk后面应该跟of doing，而不是to do

比较结构： as + adj + as + 从句          其中后一个 as是连词，后面应该加一个分句。该分句可以省略修辞。

例如我们常说的 Aaron is as tall as Bill. = Aaron is as tall as Bill is tall. = Aaron is tall. + Bill is tall. 其中主句的is tall 和分句的is tall 充当相同的句子成分(谓语宾语)，省略修辞时，形态完全相同的相同句子成分可以完全省略后者。

本题：Substances that it will 单复数不匹配，若认为匹配则指代不清，若您的方法是尝试读懂句意的您会说：改变了句子原先的逻辑。因此剩下ABD三项

把他们还原为完整的比较结构叫：

A= the toxic substances present as little risk to cause... as one (substance) in a million chances presents risk to cause... 什么叫 one substance in a million chances? 

B= the toxic substances present as little risk of causing... as one chance in a million (chances) presents risk of causing... 

one chance in a million chances presents a risk of causing cancer. 听到这句话，你就懂了：that is to say the risk is one millionth ! 百万分之一的风险。 所以正确。

D= the toxic substances present a risk (that is) as little as one chance in a million (chances) for causing...

= the risk for causing... is as little as one chance in a million (chances) for causing... is little.

"the risk for causing... is little." + "one chance in a million chances for causing is little."

"one chance is little" ??

换句话说，D项是把risk is little 和 one chance is little 作比较。一个是具体的chance 一个是抽象的risk，那个little应该意思不同吧？ 就好比说 the single bachelor is as rich as the soil is rich. 两个rich不一样吧？
