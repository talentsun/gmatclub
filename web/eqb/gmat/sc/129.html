Cajuns speak a dialect brought to southern Louisiana by the 4,000 Acadians who migrated there in 1755; their language is basically seventeenth-century French to which has been added English, Spanish, and Italian words.
A.to which has been added English, Spanish, and Italian words
B.added to which is English, Spanish, and Italian words
C.to which English, Spanish, and Italian words have been added
D.with English, Spanish, and Italian words having been added to it
E.and, in addition, English, Spanish, and Italian words are added

Their languate is French to which … words have been added倒装。正常语序应该是English, Spanish and Italian words have been added to French

  1. has 不能修饰复数words； 倒装语序很笨拙； 此处has不是修饰french
  2. is 不能修饰words；added的位置不合适，使得which的指代不清晰; 并且这个动作已经完成了，需要现在完成时 have been added
  3. BINGO ;正确的动词形式
  4. with不能很好的修饰french, 原意想表达这些语言被添加到了法语中; having been added 不能作定语修饰words；it 指代不清
  5. 失去了 French 和English, Spanish, and Italian words之间的关系，将从属结构改成并列不合理; 并且这个动作发生在过去而且完成了，需要现在完成时 have been added
