In 1850, Lucretia Mott published her Discourse on Women, arguing in a treatise for women to have equal political and legal rights and for changes in the married women's property laws.
A.arguing in a treatise for women to have equal political and legal rights
B.arguing in a treatise for equal political and legal rights for women
C.a treatise that advocates women's equal political and legal rights
D.a treatise advocating women's equal political and legal rights
E.a treatise that argued for equal political and legal rights for women

　　E the best for equal…和for changes平行，本题做的时候只注意到了for women以为不平行，中了圈套，真狡猾。

　　C选项不平行，也就是advocate的and前后用法不一致。

　　此题看完题目应该发现一个原文给的暗示点for changes in the married women's property laws　因此快速扫五个选项　没有FOR的前部死菜　剩下ABE　AB比较像就合起来和E相比　发现最大的区别是开头　AB的表达其实都没错　可是和E相比起来在意思的表达上就没有什么区别　还是说treatise 　因此AB没有E好　选E

如果B和E不知道怎样选，B是一个定语从句W+be省略的句型，E也是一个定语从句。这个定语从句出现在这个位置，前面有两个名词，一个是人，一个是书，于是E比B更优，因为在定语从句的引导词前面重复一次所指代的对象，是最严谨的大师句型，优选。
