The three women, liberal activists who strongly support legislation in favor of civil rights and environmental protection, have consistently received labor's unqualifying support.
A.have consistently received labor's unqualifying support
B.are consistently receiving the unqualifying support of labor
C.have consistently received the unqualified support of labor
D.receive consistent and unqualified support by labor
E.are receiving consistent and unqualified support by labor

题目释义：The three women, …, have consistently received….

选项分析

  • A.unqualifying使没有资格的，意思不对，应该用unqualified绝对的(used for emphasizing that a quality is complete and total)，记住unqualified support。N's n或n of N，都可以，能表达清楚意思又简洁的为上，这里labor's后面已经有一个unqualified这种不是专门为了组成名词短语而存在的形容词（如vocal tract）了，所以还是用n of N比较清晰。
  • B. are consistently receiving本身没有问题，但和原句have consistently received的时态不符，一般没有特殊原因不改变原句的逻辑意思，时态等。除非有很明显的标志，有必改不可的原因，另consistently本身有continuing to happen or develop in the same way的意思，和现在进行时are receiving语义上重复，不可取。；unqualifying词义错误。
  • C. correct。
  • D. 时态问题。错误的用consistent修饰support改变原句含义；by用在被动语态里后面才跟行为者.
  • E. 错误的用consistent修饰support改变原句含义。

补充说明

Prep08版着实有几个地方强调不更改A句的含义，基本可以有这样的结论：如果A句逻辑含义合理，则没有特殊原因(比如与A句逻辑含义相同的选项都有明显错误)，那么就不可以随意更改句意。
