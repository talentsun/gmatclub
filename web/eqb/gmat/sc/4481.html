Many Lithuanian words preserve important features of Proto-lndoeuropean, the language from which it is descended, and scholars compare it to the corresponding word in other Indoeuropean languages in order to reconstruct the original Proto-lndoeuropean form.
A.it is descended, and scholars compare it to the corresponding word in other Indoeuropean languages
B.it descends, and scholars compare it to the corresponding words in another Indoeuropean language
C.they are descended, and scholars compare them with the corresponding words in other Indoeuropean languages
D.they descend, and scholars compare them to the corresponding word in another Indoeuropean language
E.there is descent, and scholars compare them with corresponding words in other Indoeuropean languages

A选项：it按逻辑应该指代Proto-Indoeuropean，而一句话中的代词指代内容需一样，因此在后句“compare it to..."中，意思应为“Proto-Indoeuropean compare to word"，逻辑意思不对。

B选项：分析同A。另other 比another更符合句意。

C选项：they应该指代words，同位语正确语序就是“words are decended from the language"符合逻辑意思

D选项：be descended from 由。。。遗传下来。另another错误

E选项：同位语the language from there is descent，错误。
