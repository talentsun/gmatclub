Kennedy high school ranked in the academic top ten of all high schools in the state and graduated 100 percent of its students.
A.Over a decade starting in 1971,
B.For almost a decade having its start in 1971,
C.Starting in 1971 for a decade,
D.Starting for a decade in 1971
E.During a decade, a period starting in 1971，

A: over  表示涵盖了10年。B： wordy。 C：表示强调了开始的动作，而不是强调10年。D： wordy and redundant。 E:同上。 GMAT 的语法个人感觉和国家地理的阅读文章难度基本一致，这种中级难度的基本是看语感。
