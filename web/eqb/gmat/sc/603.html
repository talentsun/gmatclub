Although improved efficiency in converting harvested trees into wood products may reduce harvest rates, it will stimulate demand by increasing supply and lowering prices, thereby boosting consumption.
A.in converting harvested trees into wood products may reduce harvest rates, it will stimulate demand by increasing supply and lowering prices, thereby boosting
B.In converting harvested trees into wood products may reduce harvest rates, demand will be stimulated because of increasing supply and lowering prices, which boost
C.Of converting harvested trees into wood products may reduce harvest rates, it will stimulate demand by increasing supply and lowering prices, which boosts
D.Of harvested trees being converted into wood products may reduce harvest rates, it will stimulate demand, because it will increase supply and lower prices, thereby boosting
E.When harvested trees are converted into wood products may reduce harvest rates, demand will be stimulated because of increasing supply and lowering prices, which boost

个人认为以前的正确答案解释错的太夸张了，就先删掉了。

 

这道题A应该是没什么问题的，主句结构是：

altough efficiency may reduce sth, it (还是efficiency) will...., thereby boosting...

意思是：尽管提高了的效率也许减少了对树木的砍伐率，可是它将刺激需求，从而激发消费。

boost是stimulate的伴随状语，分词做状语，修饰的都是主句主语improved efficiency, it指代improved efficiency.

如果这种长句阅读上有困难，因为它是带有转折连词although的句子，大可以拆开成两个句子看，然后琢磨一下it到底该指代谁。

原先给的正确答案中的B最大的问题就是which的指代，boost comsumption这个动作要有个发出者，which指代的就应该是一个能够boost comsumption的东西，而这个东西显然不是demand will be stimulated because of increasing supply and lowering prices里面的任何一个成分，而且demand will be stimulated because of increasing supply and lowering prices本身也有问题，现在分词和过去分词的用法是英语的几大基础之一，对这个有疑惑的同学可以百度一下。

而在A里面  it will stimulate demand by increasing ...and lowering ...这里应该是动名词，通过这两个动作来实现stimulate demand.

 

补充，本人觉得这个句子用语义跟两个句子之间的关系就很容易做啦。（以下是我的做题方法）

句子重心在后面，要强调improved efficiency与后面的stimulate demand的关系，所以后面的句子最好不要用被动语句，最好用it 指代improved efficiency去阐述两者之间的关系。杀BE

看主谓：it will stimulate…没有问题

看代词：it 指代没有问题

看连词：which不可以指代整个句子（杀C）

看简洁与否：D明显太罗嗦，没有A好。
