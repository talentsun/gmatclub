Judge Bonham denied a motion to allow members of the jury to go home at the end of each day instead of to confine them to a hotel.
A.to allow members of the jury to go home at the end of each day instead of to confine them to
B.that would have allowed members of the jury to go home at the end of each day instead of confined to
C.under which members of the jury are allowed to go home at the end of each day instead of confining them in
D.that would allow members of the jury to go home at the end of each day rather than confinement in
E.to allow members of the jury to go home at the end of each day rather than be confined to

Sebastian：一般用rather than 不用instead of

 

我认为Sebastian的解释太笼统了。instead of和rather than都是正确的表达，只是rather than能适用的范围更加宽泛，因为intead of本身已经是个介词短语了，后面只能直接跟名词性短语或者名词。而在这里，to confine作为非谓语动词能充当的成分很多，语法上是没问题的。那么逻辑上看，就要判断to confine 跟谁平行了。

rather than和instead of都是平行结构的“分水岭”，那么到底A中instead of to confine them to是跟谁平行呢？因为前面不定式有两个， to allow和to go home, 而如果是跟to go home平行，to confine的主语就也是members of the jury, 那就变成members of the jury to confine them to a hotel (如果是themselves, 那A就没错，然而如果不是themselves, 我们就不知道到底谁被confine在酒店里了）

而如果是跟to allow平行，那么motion to allow members ...instead of to confine...逻辑意思上不平行，allow和confine并不能成为一对对比。confine和go home才是对比。因此A错了。

因此这道题选E, 因为是motion to allow members to go home...rather than (members) be confined
