Warning that computers in the United States are not secure, the NationalAcademy of Sciences has urged the nation to revamp computer security procedures, institute new emergency response teams, creating a special nongovernment organization to take charge of computer security planning.
A.creating a special nongovernment organization to take
B.creating a special nongovernment organization that takes
C.creating a special nongovernment organization for taking
D.and create a special nongovernment organization for taking
E.and create a special nongovernment organization to take

分析：考察平行；to revamp, take，institute；

A.三个平行需要and；creating无法与institute平行，且creating分词修饰前面的句子，改变句意；

B.三个平行需要and；creating无法与institute平行，且creating分词修饰前面的句子，改变句意；that指代还未诞生的organization错误的替换了主语；

C.三个平行需要and；creating无法与institute平行，且creating分词修饰前面的句子，改变句意；for taking不准确，to take表达目的；

D.for taking不准确，to take表达目的；

E.Correct；

 

  1. 三个元素平行，最后一个元素之前加and。
  2. 此题的三个元素不用都重复to因为这样表示不会产生歧义。（OG）
