In an effort to reduce their inventories, Italian vintners have cut prices; their wines have been priced to sell, and they are.
A.have been priced to sell, and they are
B.are priced to sell, and they have
C.are priced to sell, and they do
D.are being priced to sell, and have
E.had been priced to sell, and they have

1. 考察省略。只有完全相同的成分才能省略。A, sell和selling，不能省略。B, sell和sold，不能省略。C, 正确。D, 同B。E, 同B。

2. being和过去完成时，降低选择优先级

3. 逗号与and的用法:只有两个主句主语相同，且后一个主句中有前一个主句的主语，才需要在and前加逗号。D中后一句没有主语，所以不需要加逗号
