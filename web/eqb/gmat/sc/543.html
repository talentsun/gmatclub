A.prevented from dissipating its acoustic energy as a result of
B.prevented from having its acoustic energy dissipated by

这里用的是独立主格。

独立主格的例句是（根据百度百科）：

1. There being no taxis, we had to walk.

2. The work done, we went home.

E之所以错是因为，阻止energy dissipating的是boundaries，而不是sound。
